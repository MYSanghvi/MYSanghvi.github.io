About Our Site

What is Public Relations (PR)?

The role PR plays in your business is to shape the public view of your company. This is done through customer interactions, social media outlets and earned media as well as news stories, interviews and features about your company, its products and services.

Why do you need PR?

PR is important to have a competitive advantage of having a favourable image in the minds of your target audience. Public Relations adds the human touch to your marketing strategy. If people follow you of your brand, you need PR.

What is the difference between Marketing, Advertising and PR?

Marketing is the umbrella, while advertising and public relations are the subsets. Advertising is for actively making a product or service known to the audience. Public relations is the maintenance of a favourable public image of your company.
